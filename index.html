Bio

Alyssa (or Ysa, pronounced Ee-suh) is a dedicated and an ambitious professional computer engineer working in one of the top wireless communications companies in the world. She is a graduate of the Verizon Leadership Development Program, a rotational program wherein she gained leadership experience managing teams big and small as well as technical, end-to-end network experiences. This select curriculum allowed her to develop a range of skills across both the wireless and wireline business facets of the company. Her favorite roles and projects during her tenure in VLDP included leading a cellular network operations team in Houston, physically installing and maintaining fiber optic services in Boston (a job that also included hands-on customer service), experimenting with different system architectures in a lab setting, and designing a small cell network in landmarks and popular attractions in the Dallas/Fort Worth metro area, most notably at the Cowboys Stadium in Arlington, TX.

As a lifelong student, Ysa grows by collaborating with people that continually challenge her to acquire new skills as well as advance her current skill set. She is a kinesthetic learner who enjoys working with her hands and thrives in a highly innovative, fast-paced environment that feeds her creative and technical side while providing day-to-day engineering opportunities. Because she grew up in a developing country, Ysa is inspired to help create solutions that improve the human condition and understands the importance of using science, technology, and engineering to accomplish this goal. She hopes to one day make her mark in the development of both hardware and software engineering of emerging technologies such as Internet of Things (IoT) devices. As someone who continues to further her own education, Ysa would love to give back to the community and inspire other young women in the Philippines to pursue STEM careers.

Ysa is at the end of her journey into becoming a full-fledged full stack developer through the flex program of the University of Texas at Austin. She has developed web applications that includes a word-guessing game, a user-friendly public transportation scheduler, and a tool that checks and alerts users of the allergens and additives of food products.

Ysa obtained her undergraduate degree of a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at the University of Minnesota-Twin Cities in 2012 and was an active member of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers organization while in college. She received her Project Management certification with the University of Houston in 2016 as she aspires to hone and balance both technical and soft skills.

Ysa currently lives in a suburb of Houston and is actively seeking employment in Dallas, Texas to relocate to be closer to her partner. She enjoys traveling to different places and experiencing other cultures and cuisines. To date, Ysa has visited Iceland, the Netherlands, Korea, Japan, India, Canada, Mexico, Colombia, and Peru. Her most recent adventure was a four-day hike to conquer the majestic Machu Picchu. Ysa considers the accomplishment one of her proudest moments in life. When not programming or travelling the world, Ysa practices the acoustic guitar and enjoys spending time with her family and her cat, Nala.
